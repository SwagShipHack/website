What do we do?
In short: store, pack and distribute a range of swag for online events and devrel. We have experience doing this for a range of events, large and small and we're run by a seasoned hackathon organiser who knows what makes events tick.
We take the strain of managing your mailout, while you get on with delighting attendees in the way you know best.
We can:
  • Store, pack and ship your swag
  • Design & print custom packaging and mailers

  • Store and dropship custom orders

Swag shipping for events

We can help! Retaining that physical connection with attendees is important, and we know how delightful a selection of goodies can be. It can really make the difference! It's also a great way to engage attendees with your sponsors and another option you can add to your sponsor deck.

How does it work?

You ship us your swag, or arrange for your suppliers and sponsors to ship to us, tell us what you want in each swag pack and let us know who we're sending it to. Then you can make a cuppa and move the Trello card over!
We use a variety of carriers and shipping methods to get the best value for your event.

How much does it cost?

This depends primarily on what you're sending. Costs for just stickers start from £1 per pack.

FAQs
Is this environmentally friendly?
There is always an environmental impact to sending items, but we work to minimise this using recycled and recyclable packaging, carriers who are working to decarbonise and by limiting our use of plastics and recycling as much as we can.
Where do you ship from?
Peterborough, UK.
Which carriers do you use?
Primarily low weight, volume shipping: Royal Mail. We also use couriers such as DHL and ParcelForce for larger items. As we ship in volume, we're able to negotiate pricing discounts.
Can you ship abroad?
Yes! We'll even do the customs paperwork for you ;)
Do you a have minimum/maximum number of items you can ship?
Not really, however we're geared more towards small to medium events.
